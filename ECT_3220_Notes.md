### I.N.F.O.

- Inner
  - 2 to 5 ft.
  - Recognize details on props
  - Recognize emotion's on faces
- Near
  - 5 to 10 ft.
  - Characters grab attention
  - Audience notices props
- Far
  - 10 to 18 feet.
  - Characters grab some attention
  - Props need to be highlighted to draw attention
- Outer
  - Beyond 18 feet.
  - Characters and props are ignored unless isolated

### Viewer Postion Types

"The Newton"
: Stationary chair viewer
: Pseudo confines viewing to 120 to 180 degs unless effort expended to turn

"The Copernicus"
: Viewer in a swivel chair
: Viewer freely rotates

### Control vs Agency

"Crew creates Control"

"Audience provides Agency"

"In every medium, the crew controls the story during its creation."
"In cine-VR, the audience has agency while the story is being experienced."
